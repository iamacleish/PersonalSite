Virtual Reality Bike

Date: November 2015

Link: Virtual Reality Bike

VRBike is a virtual reality bike riding experience built with Unity, Oculus Rift, and Arduino. Using rotary encoders we converted a stationary exercise bike into a VR experience, complete with 3D environmental sound, as well as DMX controlled wind and smells based on speed and in-game locations respectively.
