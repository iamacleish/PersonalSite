Boffer Quest is a visual novel style game with card based fighting mechanics. Enter the world of LARP and work your way up a socially awkward hierarchy by honing your strength, reputation, and alcohol tolerance. Travel to three different kingdoms to fight and meet with a myriad of characters, earning their respect, aid, and occasionally their affections as you go! In this medieval fantasy world, conflicts that cannot be solved via dialogue, will be battled out in a strategy card game format, with the results affecting the player's progression through the story. The player's actions and choices in the visual novel will affect the types of cards they have access to, and the strategies he has the ability to play, as well as the progression of the story. Go forth and discover the ins and outs of a community as varied and diverse as the medieval fantasy realm it imitates!
