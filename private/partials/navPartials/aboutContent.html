About

Recently, Ian has been delving into website development. He is currently focusing on front end web development using HTML, CSS/SCSS, Javascript, Node.js, and Bootstrap, which he used to develop the Battle for the Ring website.

Ian has been designing and playing games for most of his life. He has experience in level design, camera controllers including virtual reality, 3D modeling, gameplay mechanics and more. In addition to multiple simulations such as a virtual reality bike experience, Ian has worked on several games. These include Suddenly Words!, and Bar Crawl: A Night to Forget.
